ABOUT

Welcome to Cubed! Cubed is a personal project of mine to discover eco-friendly alternatives to common building materials!...

Ever ask yourself: Why don't we use 'insert material name here' to manufacture 'insert construction item/unit/component name here'? Well I certainly do. And one doesn't have to dig too deep to discover that you are not alone. There is a significant body of research on this topic, particularly the application of waste products within common construction units.

This is ultimately the purpose of Cubed, to explore these possibilities with the intention of creating ... yep, you guessed it, cubes capable of having real world application in the modern construction and engineering context. This section of the webpage serves as a blog of sorts, documenting my research into the subject and recording findings. Below you will find sections pertaining to my scientific research as well as documented progress with graphics, images, videos etc etc. At the same time, this page is built entirely with my newly learnt web-development skills. Win-win right?

Wrong.

Win-win-win (The Office fans will get it), you also win because you get to see the synthesis and evolution of Cubed.
